Project Title: Project Name - FS Car Rental Project

. Formal Introduction:
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
This application is a full-featured, end-to-end car rental platform. 
It’s designed to provide a premium booking experience for customers 
while offering administrators a comprehensive control center. Our goal 
is to make vehicle rentals faster, more secure, and extremely user-friendly 
through a modern, high-speed interface.

. TECHNOLOGY
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
. FRONTEND (The User Experience):
- React.js: For building a fast, scalable Single Page Application (SPA).
- Tailwind CSS & DaisyUI: For creating a stunning, modern, and mobile-friendly UI.
- React Router: For seamless navigation between different sections.
- AOS & Framer Motion: For micro-interactions and smooth animations.

. BACKEND (The Core Logic):
- Node.js & Express: A powerful, event-driven server architecture.
- MongoDB & Mongoose: A flexible NoSQL database to store car specs and bookings.
- JWT (JSON Web Tokens): Ensuring secure logins and authorized access.
- Bcrypt: Specialized for high-security password hashing.

. ADMIN VS. USER ROLES 
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
. ADMINISTRATOR CAPABILITIES:
- Overview: Monitor revenue, active bookings, and total site visitors.
- Car Management: Full control to add, edit, or delete vehicles from the system.
- User Management: Oversight of all registered accounts and their activities.
- Booking Control: Track and manage all customer reservations in real-time.

. USER CAPABILITIES:
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
- Smart Search: Browse the car with advanced filtering (by type, brand, price).
- Deep Details: View high-quality images and specific car performance data.
- Instant Booking: A streamlined booking flow with real-time status tracking.
- Personal History: Access and manage personal rental history and current status.
